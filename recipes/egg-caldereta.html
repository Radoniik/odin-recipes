Egg Caldereta

Filipinos way of Egg Cooking

Ingredients

  • 2tbls Cooking Oil
  • 1tbl Garlic
  • 1pc Onion
  • 1pc Carrot
  • 1pc Potato
  • 1cup Water
  • 0.5cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap®
  • 6pcs Egg
  • 0.5cup Pineapple Juice

How to Cook:

  1. Heat cooking oil. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant. Add carrots and potatoes. Pour in water and bring to a boil.
  2. dd tomato sauce to thicken the sauce. Season with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®
  3. Add eggs marinated in pineapple juice. Add salt and pepper to taste.
