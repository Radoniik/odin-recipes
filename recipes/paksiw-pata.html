Paksiw Pata

Delicious Pork Dish by the Filipinos

Ingredients

  • 3tbls Vegetable Oil
  • 4pcs Saba Banana
  • 1kg Pork Pata
  • 1Head Garlic
  • 0.5cup Spiced Vinegar
  • 0.5cup Soy Sauce
  • 0.5cup Brown Sugar
  • 1pc Bay Leaf
  • 1tsp Black Peppercorn
  • 0.25cup Banana Heart
  • 1sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap®

How to Cook:

  1. Sauté saging na saba in 2 tbsp of oil. Remove and set aside.
  2. Sauté pork and garlic in remaining oil. Add vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, bay leaf, peppercorn and banana blossom . Pour water to barely cover pork. Simmer until tender.
  3. Add saba and simmer for another 5 minutes. Season withMAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®. Transfer into a serving plate and serve.
